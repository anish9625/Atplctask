BIHAR

Bihar is a state in eatern India. The name Bihar is derived from the Sanskrit and Pali word "Bihara". Bihar is the 3rd largest state of India by IR population and 12th by area. It covers an area of 99,200 sq km. The largest city and capital is Patna. the official language are Hindi and Urdu. Bihar is the origin of two biggest religions in the world, namely Buddhism and Jainism. World's oldest university is Nalanda University which is present in Bihar. Nalanda library was a set on fire by the army of Bakhtiyar khilji. The library was believed to hold 9 million manuscripts and took 3 full months to burn down into ashes. Bihar has 38 administrative districts, 101 subdivisions and 534 CD blocks.
